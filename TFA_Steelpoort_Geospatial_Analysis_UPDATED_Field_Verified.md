### Appendix C: Cladode Curing Best Practices

**Standard Horticultural Protocol for *Opuntia* spp.:**
1. **Harvest:** Cut mature cladodes (pads) from mother plant
2. **Curing:** Place in shaded, dry location for 10-30 days
3. **Purpose:** 
   - Callus formation on cut surface (prevents rot)
   - Moisture reduction (50-70% water content ‚Üí 30-50%)
   - Pathogen resistance (cut surface dries, hardens)
4. **Duration:** Climate-dependent
   - Arid regions: 10-14 days
   - Semi-arid (Steelpoort): **21 days** (per TFA protocol)
   - Humid regions: 30+ days
5. **Indicators:** Ready when cut surface is dry, slightly hardened, no exudate
